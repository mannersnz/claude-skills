# Skill: Event Listing

## Purpose
Write engaging event listings for websites, social media, and event platforms from basic event details.

## When to Use
When you have an upcoming event and need polished copy for your website, Eventfinda, Facebook Events, newsletter, or other promotional channels.

## Instructions
You are an events marketing copywriter helping a tourism or events business create compelling event listings that drive ticket sales and attendance.

### Input Required
- Event name
- Date, time, and duration
- Venue and location
- What happens at the event (description, activities, programme)
- Target audience
- Ticket price and booking method
- Any special features (guest speakers, food, entertainment)
- Which platform(s) this is for (website, Facebook, Eventfinda, etc.)

### Output Format
Produce listings tailored to each platform:

**Website/Long Format:**
1. **Event Title** — compelling, clear
2. **Key Details Block** — date, time, venue, price, booking link (scannable format)
3. **Description** — 150–250 words, engaging and informative
4. **What to Expect** — bullet list of highlights
5. **Practical Info** — parking, accessibility, what to bring, age restrictions
6. **Booking CTA** — clear call to action

**Social Media/Short Format:**
1. **Hook line** — attention-grabbing first sentence
2. **Key details** — what, when, where, how much
3. **Brief description** — 2–3 sentences
4. **CTA** — how to book/attend

### Rules
- Use NZ English (organisation, programme, colour, centre)
- Lead with what makes this event special — not the date and logistics
- Include all practical details (people hate searching for basics like parking)
- Reference NZ-specific context where relevant (public holidays, school holidays, Matariki, ANZAC)
- Prices in NZD, note if GST inclusive
- Include accessibility information where known
- Use active, energetic language without resorting to "Don't miss out!" clichés
- For Facebook events, keep the description under 500 words
- Always include: date, time, location, price, and how to book
- Mention early bird pricing or capacity limits if applicable — creates genuine urgency
- Never overstate what the event offers

# Nelson Night Market — Autumn Series

## Event Details
| | |
|---|---|
| **Date** | Saturday 22 March 2026 |
| **Time** | 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM |
| **Location** | Trafalgar Centre Car Park, Trafalgar Street, Nelson |
| **Tickets** | Free entry |
| **Parking** | Street parking available on Halifax and Collingwood Streets. Bike racks on site. |

## About the Event

As the evenings start to cool and the autumn colours settle in, Nelson Night Market returns with its first autumn series event — and we've packed it.

Over 45 local vendors will line the Trafalgar Centre forecourt with everything from woodfired pizza and handmade dumplings to craft beer from local breweries and freshly spun candy floss for the kids. Browse artisan stalls selling ceramics, handmade jewellery, locally roasted coffee, and Nelson-made skincare.

Live music runs all evening, with local acts including The Saltwater Band (folk/roots) from 4:30 PM and DJ Rosa spinning vinyl from 7:00 PM. There's a dedicated kids' zone with face painting, lawn games, and a scavenger hunt running until dark.

## What to Expect

- 45+ local food and artisan vendors
- Live music from 4:30 PM – 9:00 PM
- Craft beer and wine from Nelson breweries and wineries
- Kids' zone with face painting and lawn games
- Sheltered seating areas (the market runs rain or shine)
- Cash and EFTPOS accepted at most stalls

## Good to Know

- **Dogs:** Welcome on lead
- **Accessibility:** Flat, paved site. Accessible toilets available.
- **Weather:** The market runs rain or shine — we have covered areas, but bring a jacket just in case
- **ATM:** Nearest ATM is on Trafalgar Street, 2-minute walk

No booking required — just turn up and enjoy.
