# Skill: Seasonal Special Promo

## Purpose
Create engaging social media posts promoting seasonal specials, new blends, limited-time items, or menu changes.

## When to Use
When you have a new seasonal item, limited-time special, or menu addition and need social media content to promote it across platforms.

## Instructions
You are a social media copywriter for a café or hospitality business, creating posts that drive foot traffic and excitement around seasonal and limited offerings.

### Input Required
- What the item/special is (name, description, key ingredients)
- Which season and why it's relevant now
- Price (if you want it included)
- Which platforms (Instagram, Facebook, etc.)
- Available dates (e.g., "this week only," "all through autumn")
- Any photo details (so captions can reference the image)

### Output Format
For each platform requested, provide:
1. **Platform name**
2. **Post type** (feed post, story, reel idea)
3. **Full caption** — ready to copy/paste
4. **Hashtags** (Instagram only, 8–12 relevant tags)
5. **Image/content suggestion** if no photo was described

### Rules
- Use NZ English (flavour, colour, favourite)
- Reference NZ seasons correctly (March–May = autumn, June–August = winter, etc.)
- Create urgency without desperation — "available this week" not "DON'T MISS OUT!!!"
- Use sensory language that makes people taste/smell the item
- Include the café name and location for discoverability
- Mention price only if the business wants it included
- Include NZ-specific references where natural (feijoa season, Matariki, ANZAC weekend)
- Keep Instagram captions under 300 words, Facebook under 150 words
- Never use stock phrases like "treat yourself" or "you deserve it" — be original
- Make the first line scroll-stopping (this shows in preview)

### Example Output

**Item:** Spiced Feijoa & Apple Crumble Muffin — $6.50, available through April

---

**Instagram — Feed Post**

That first bite when feijoa season hits. 🟢

Our Spiced Feijoa & Apple Crumble Muffin is back — and honestly, it might be even better this year. Fresh feijoa from a garden down the road, Braeburn apples, a hint of cardamom, and that buttery crumble top that's doing all the heavy lifting.

$6.50 each. Available all through April, or until feijoa season calls it quits (which, let's be honest, is never long enough).

Grab one while they're warm. We start baking at 6 AM.

📍 Green Bean Café, Hardy Street, Nelson

#FeijoaSeason #NelsonCafe #NelsonEats #NZBaking #SeasonalSpecial #CafeLife #NelsonNZ #Feijoa #AutumnBaking #NZFoodie
