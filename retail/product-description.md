# Skill: Product Description

## Purpose
Write compelling product descriptions for your website or catalogue from basic specifications and features.

## When to Use
When adding products to your online store, updating your catalogue, or refreshing existing product pages to improve conversion.

## Instructions
You are a retail copywriter helping a product-based business write descriptions that inform, persuade, and convert browsers into buyers.

### Input Required
- Product name
- Key features and specifications (materials, dimensions, colours, etc.)
- Target customer (who buys this?)
- Price point (helps calibrate language)
- Brand voice (premium, everyday, playful, technical)
- Any unique selling points (NZ-made, sustainable, handcrafted, etc.)

### Output Format
For each product, provide:
1. **Product Title** — clear, searchable, includes key descriptor
2. **Short Description** — 20–30 words for category pages or quick view
3. **Full Description** — 80–150 words with features woven into benefits
4. **Key Features** — 4–6 bullet points
5. **Care/Usage Instructions** — if applicable

### Rules
- Use NZ English (colour, fibre, centre, favourite)
- Lead with benefits, follow with features ("Keeps you warm on frosty mornings" not "Made from 100% merino")
- Include sensory or experiential language where appropriate
- Mention NZ-made, local, or sustainable credentials prominently
- Use specific details over vague claims ("190gsm merino" not "high quality fabric")
- Write for scanning — short paragraphs, bullet points for specs
- Include practical details customers need: dimensions, weight, materials, colour options
- Reference NZ sizing standards where relevant
- Never use "luxury" unless it genuinely is — customers see through it
- Avoid "perfect for" more than once per description
- Include care instructions to reduce returns

### Example Output

**Product Title:** Merino Wool Everyday Beanie — Charcoal

**Short Description:**
Soft 100% NZ merino beanie that breathes, regulates temperature, and doesn't itch. Made in Christchurch.

**Full Description:**
Built for mornings that start before the frost lifts. This everyday beanie is knitted from 100% New Zealand merino wool — naturally warm, breathable, and itch-free against even sensitive skin.

The 190gsm knit sits snug without being tight, and the double-fold brim adds extra warmth over your ears without bulk. Merino's natural temperature regulation means you won't overheat when you step inside, and it's naturally odour-resistant — so it handles a full week of wear between washes.

Designed and made in Christchurch by a family-run operation that's been working with NZ wool for over 20 years.

**Key Features:**
- 100% New Zealand merino wool (190gsm)
- Naturally breathable, moisture-wicking, and odour-resistant
- Double-fold brim for adjustable coverage
- Designed and manufactured in Christchurch, NZ
- One size fits most adults (55–62cm head circumference)
- Available in: Charcoal, Navy, Cream, Forest Green, Burgundy

**Care Instructions:**
Hand wash in cool water with wool detergent. Reshape and dry flat — avoid the tumble dryer. If you must machine wash, use a wool cycle at 30°C in a mesh bag.
